My Journey

From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by how people interact with technology. Whether it’s a sleek mobile app or a user-friendly website, I believe that great design can enhance our lives. Over the past two years, I’ve immersed myself in the world of UX, learning the ropes and experimenting with different approaches.
